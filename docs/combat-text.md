# Combat Text

ScuffedHolograms can customise the floating **damage numbers** that appear during combat (their size, colour, duration, and animation style) for everyone on the server. This is separate from holograms. It restyles the game's built-in combat text.

Open it from `/sch menu`, then the **Combat Text** tab.

## Enabling

Combat Text customisation is **off by default**, leaving the game's normal damage numbers untouched. Flip the **Enable** toggle to **ON** to apply your settings. Turning it back **OFF** restores the vanilla damage numbers. Changes apply right away, with no restart needed.

## Presets

Three presets set everything at once, and you can then fine-tune the individual values:

| Preset | Style |
|--------|-------|
| **MapleStory** | Large, bold orange numbers that pop and rise. The flashy default. |
| **Vanilla** | Matches the game's built-in look (white, smaller, quick) |
| **Minimal** | Smaller, subtle light-grey numbers |

## Fine-tuning

After picking a preset you can adjust:

| Setting | Range | Default |
|---------|-------|---------|
| **Font size** | 20 to 200 | 85 |
| **Duration** | 0.2 to 3.0 seconds | 0.9 |
| **Text colour** | any colour | orange |

**Reset to Defaults** returns the values to the Vanilla style. **Apply** saves and applies your current settings live.

> Combat Text is a single server-wide style. It applies to all players, not per-hologram.
